Denizli is a city in western Turkey, located in the Aegean Region. It is the capital of Denizli Province and has a population of around 575,000 people. Denizli is known for its thermal baths and spas, and is a major center for the production of textiles and other goods. The city is also home to several universities and other educational institutions. Denizli is located in a region with a rich history and cultural heritage, and there are several historical and cultural attractions in and around the city, including the ancient city of Hierapolis, which is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
